Biography

I am a PhD student in Computer Science at Graduate Center of CUNY. Prior to CUNY, I received my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from University of Science and Technology of China, Hefei, China, in 2009. My current research interests include High Performance Geospatial Computing using GPGPUs, Big Spatial Data Processing, Spatial Databases, and GIS applications. My advisor is Prof. Jianting Zhang
